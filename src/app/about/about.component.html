Who is Martin Handford?

English illustrator Martin Handford, best known for his Where's Wally books. Known as Where's Waldo in America, it is a puzzle picture book that challenges the reader to search for Wally(Waldo) on the page. Wally is always in a red and white striped shirt with a matching bobble hat and glasses. He is hidden amongst throngs of people doing funny things at a location that can range from the beach to space. Where's Wally got so popular that an animated series was created after the books, a comic strip, and a series of video games. Handford describes Wally as a world traveler and time travel aficionado and has a dog named Woof with him. His friends can also be found in the books; Wilma, Wenda, Wizard Whitebeard and The Wally Watchers. He even has a nemesis, Odlaw. (Wikipedia contributors, 2021)
